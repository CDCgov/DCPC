CLEW Instance of the LAPPS Grid

This information is geared towards the expert user

You can develop new pipelines to compare the quality of output from using different combinations of tools, components, and features. CLEW integrates the use of LIF from the LAPPS Grid to provide interoperability so you can integrate different clinical tools, components, and features across NLP frameworks.

The primary users of the CLEW instance of the LAPPS Grid are information technology (IT) specialists. These specialists should have graduate-level expertise in machine learning, computational linguistics, and software engineering. The team should also include an expert with medical knowledge.
