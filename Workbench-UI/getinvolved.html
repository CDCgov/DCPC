Get Involved

The CLEW is not the development of a new NLP or machine-learning tool, but the development of a platform to harness all of the great work from across the clinical community. The sharing of tools, pipelines, and services will enable the clinical community to learn from one another, brainstorm ways to improve outcomes, and reduce duplication of work. There are several ways to get involved with CLEW, including:

  • Presenting your work on a scheduled webinar
  • Sharing a new tool on CLEW that has been useful in your work
  • Uploading and publishing your pre-developed and tested pipelines and language Models for other to use and test with their own data
  • Share documentation, resources or publications on new methods, best practices, or Lessons learned for others to learn from
